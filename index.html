Privacy Policy for HOMAGIC Architect

Last updated: May 23, 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: email address and name when you create an account
  • Photos: room, exterior, and garden photos you upload for AI redesign
  • Usage data: number of designs generated, design history
  • Anonymous usage: if you use the app as a guest, a random anonymous ID is assigned — no personal data is collected

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI-powered interior and exterior design suggestions
  • To manage your subscription and weekly design quota
  • To save and display your design history

3. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — authentication and data storage
  • Google Gemini AI — AI image generation
  • fal.ai — AI image generation (fallback provider)
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Google Play — payment processing

4. Data Storage

Your photos and designs are stored securely on Supabase servers in the EU (Frankfurt region). We do not sell your data to third parties.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time via Settings → Delete Account in the app.
